Hell On Tap is a game I've created from scratch using HTML, CSS, Javascript/JQuery, Aseprite, Photoshop, beepbox.co
and fonts.google.com.

I started Hell On Tap in September 16 of 2018.

Hell On Tap is a game about being a bartender who owes a debt to the Devil. To pay off this debt, the protagonist
finds himself in the pits of Hell serving drinks to various monsters who inhabit the dark and bleak nightmare. The
objective will be to make enough Demon Coins to buy your way back to your normal life. There will be many obstacles
along the way to keep the player from reaching their goal. Obstacles such as a Hellish cat who comes to the bar
after the long hours at their salaried profession to sing horrible karaoke that keeps your repuation down or your
Supervisor Grangor, who will jump on top of the bar and swing his axe at your neck.

But whether you're being literally pulled further into Hell by the hand of Satan's faceless assistant or having the
Grim Reaper visit your dreams to take your life away, there will be many chances to earn the money and reputation
needed to be successful and return to your life on Earth.
